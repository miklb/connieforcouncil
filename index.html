Connie for Council

Appoint Connie Burton to Tampa City Council

Connie Burton will be a balancing force that can genuinely advocate for sensible policies, primarily through budget reform, awarding of City of Tampa contracts, observation of development connected to rational and rational policies that aren't overwhelming to established residents, and making sure that the City Council Representatives exercise the rights, duties, and responsibilities the People entrusted and elected us to perform.

Who is Connie Burton?

A VOICE OF ALL VOICES!

  • Daughter, Mother, Grandmother, Great Grandmother
  • Over 40 years of experience
  • Community Activist
  • Local Champion of The Poor And Working Class
  • Strong Advocate
  • Accomplished Representative
  • National Spokesperson
  • Stands up For What's Right
  • Passionate & Compassionate

In her own words.

“We are confronted with active forces that are adamant about reversing the gains of the Civil Rights Movement. Ironically, since the departure of Yvonne Yolie Capin from Tampa City Council, Tampa City Council has not had any female representatives. The six men that make up the current membership of Tampa City Council are all fair minded and dedicated but the addition of one female voice will certainly add a degree of diversity that is much needed.”

This is a grassroots volunteer led site to encourage fellow Tampa residents to advocate to their city council members to appoint Connie Burton to the vacant seat for District 3 at-large.
