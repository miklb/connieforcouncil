How to Help
Appoint Connie Burton to Tampa City Council

First, thank you for your interest in advocating for Connie. We are providing a Google form you can submit which we will print and deliver to Council the morning of April 5 before they vote. We also encourage you to contact your city council members (links to come) and let them know why they should vote for Connie.

This is a grassroots volunteer led site to encourage fellow Tampa residents to advocate to their city council members to appoint Connie Burton to the vacant seat for District 3 at-large.
